Who are we?

A Group of people who are interested in tech with a dream of building a community full of tech enthusiasts






We follow the 3# principle


#ASK

#LEARN

#DO

































ASK

Asking people your doubts is a really good habit.It helps you get knowledge about the things you don't know,though we still encourage people to google out their doubts before asking others which will help them to also learn new things while solving their doubts at the same time making them self-dependant.









LEARN

Asking alone wouldn't help you solve your problems.You should learn from your mistakes.Do some research about the topic you are interested in and always try to always be updated in the category of your interest.Don't waste your time on things that are completely outdated and are not going to be useful.









DO

Knowing all the recipes and not making any dish is again a waste of knowledge and time.If you don't implement the knowledge you have then there is no use of that knowledge.Try to build something with all of what resources you have,you may be surprised by knowing your own potential.









What are we going to do?



As mentioned above our main goal is to build a community of people interested in tech.This will be a platform where people are freely able to express their ideas.



People can communicate and learn from each other.There are no age restrictions anyone interested can join the program when fully iniated.We are thinking of keeping live streams with engineering people where children and all the people can ask them their questions.We also will be keeping training camps for people interested in robotics and automation(Starting on a small scale)



This is just an idea that we want to execute and so we need support from you.This is an initiative to help nurture the budding developers of future.









Tinkering Labs?Rethink



Tinkering Labs are a great initiative but people are not making use of it to the full extent.The concept was actually very good but was completely built on sand as can be seen with regards to current situations of these labs.Poor maintainance is the one of the major reasons for this.It takes a hell lot of time for a good idea to appear but because it is not properly maintained everything goes waste.We will not let this happen.We will regularly keep sessions and there will be atleast one person incharge of handling all the activties even if anyone is busy somebody will be there.
